Welcome to the
Snake Game





Following are the instructions of playing the game :

  • Use the arrow keys to move the snake.
  • There are 6 levels and there are minimum points to pass a level.
  • When you get the required minimum points,you will automatically be asked for progressing onto next level
  • Do keep in mind that the snake cannot cross the boundaries.
  • The snake cannot cross the walls.
  • If you hit a wall or boundary,the game will be over
