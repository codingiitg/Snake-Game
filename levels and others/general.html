• Use ARROW KEYS for movement of snake.
  • There are 3 types of Foods :-
    • Red colored food with 10 points each.
    • Blue colored food with 15 points each.
    • Yellow colored food with 20 points each.
  • Press Spacebar to pause/resume the game.
  • Dont hit a boundary or a Snake else the game will be over
  • You have to achieve a minimum score (which will be displayed below the game screen) to pass a level.



Press OK button to start the game
